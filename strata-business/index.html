How It Works

In, organized, done

Five steps from paper to a structured, AI-readable archive. No manual filing, no misfiled records, no decisions to make. Documents come in, get read, get classified, get filed — and Nexus answers questions across all of it.

01
Capture
Scan paper documents with a phone or drop digital files into the incoming folder. Mail, invoices, contracts, statements — anything physical or digital enters the same pipeline.
02
Read
Text extraction reads every word from every page. Handwritten notes, stamped forms, and low-quality scans are image-preserved alongside the extracted text.
03
Classify
Nexus identifies the document type, the client or vendor it belongs to, the date, and any action items — renewals, payment due dates, outstanding balances.
04
File
Every document gets a precise structured filename and lands in the right folder automatically. No manual decisions. No misfiled records.
05
Answer
Ask Nexus anything about your archive in plain language. He searches across every filed document and returns the answer — with the source document identified.
