How It Works

Scan it in, walk away

Five steps from physical paper to a structured, AI-readable archive. No manual filing. No decisions to make.

01
Capture
Scan a stack of documents with your phone. Auto-capture fires as you move pages — no buttons to tap.
02
Transfer
Files sync automatically from phone to your local machine over your home network. Nothing leaves your environment.
03
Read
OCR extracts text from every page. Documents with handwriting or stamps are image-preserved alongside the extracted text.
04
Classify
The AI reads the text, identifies what each document is, who it belongs to, and any action items that need attention.
05
Name & File
Every document gets a precise structured filename — findable by a human in Finder or an AI searching programmatically.

The Platform

A data vault built for AI to reason over

Strata is more than a filing system. It's a structured private data layer — designed from the ground up to be read, searched, and understood by an AI assistant that knows you.

Strata stores everything. An AI agent on top can answer questions across all of it.

Data Layer
Strata — every document, photo, health record, and communication ingested, classified, and permanently stored on your hardware.
Intelligence Layer
A local AI agent that reads your vault and answers questions across years of stored records — privately, on your machine, with no data leaving your environment.

Ask where a document is. Ask what your insurance covers. Ask what was happening in your life in 2019. The vault has the answer — the agent surfaces it.

The Vision

Every document you scan, every photo you ingest, every record you preserve — that's raw material. And raw material compounds.
